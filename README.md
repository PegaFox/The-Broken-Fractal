#### Manilla room

- Randomly appears in level 0
- Calming aura
- Different wallpaper
- Single, orange-tinted light in ceiling
- Occasional blackouts
- Prolonged stay can lead to level 1 or 2
- Sounds from behind walls?
- MEG guide on center table
- May have a chair
- Up to 4 entrances
- Can meet with others, may spawn with explorers in it

### Level 1 notes:

- Low hanging fog
- Occasional puddles caused by fog
- Puddle water is safe to drink (apart from standard stagnant-water dangers)
- Concrete floors and walls
- Has staircases, elevators, isolated rooms, and hallways
- Random containers of supplies appear and disappear
- Useful supplies include food, Almond Water, batteries, tarps, weaponry(mostly melee/low-tech), clothing, and medical supplies
- Junk supplies include assorted car parts, boxes of crayons, used syringes, partially burned paper, live mice, mice in a catatonic state that have been injected with unknown substances, shoelaces, loose change, and bundles of human hair
- Graffiti that changes/disappears when out of view
- Lighting fails occasionally
- Entities may appear/disappear when out of view
- Extra entities roam during light failures
- Entities include: Plague goblins, Vent arms, Clumps, Crawlers, Dullers, Growlers, Hounds, Nguithr'xurh, Skin stealers, Smilers, Wranglers, and Wretches
- Traveling far enough down straight hallways can lead to level 2
- Noclipping near paintings can lead to Party level
- Doors, sometimes with an exit sign above them, can lead to the Corridors

#### MEG Base Alpha

- Large base with good fortifications
- Close to an entrance
- Sends out scouting parties to help newcomers

#### BNTG Trader Keep

- Approximately 412 inhabitants
- A large market for trading supplies
- Inside the Storage Hall
- Friendly with MEG
- More crates appear in this area

#### BNTG Trader Vault

- Storage area for BNTG
- Heavy security
- Items randomly disappear/reappear between storage lockers

#### Tom's diner

- Shop and social space where food can be bought

#### The Corridors

- Smaller halls
- Mazelike layout
- No supply crates
- No entity spawns
- Random rooms with decoration can have limited supplies (eg. Office rooms, Hospital infirmaries)
